Jumpaï's story

Jumpaï is a free platformer game where anyone can create their own levels and play them online with the community. Started as a hobby project back in november 2016, Jumpaï has now a serious ambition of claiming it's spot among the top creator games. Sooner or later, Jumpaï will shine!

My name is Alexander Winter and I am founder and co-owner of Jumpaï. I study Computer Science at McGill University and am glad to constantly benefit the project from my newly acquired knowledge. There's no doubt that Jumpaï is very important to me considering the time and effort I've put so far in this project.
