Welcome to MakerKing’s press kit. On this page you should find any info you need to post articles or to learn about MakerKing. If you need any additional information let us know and we will make it available.

Description

MakerKing is a platformer focused on level creation and sharing. With the integrated level editor, anyone can create levels in a couple of minutes and upload it to play online with their friends. All finished levels are available in the level browser, granting players an ever growing catalog of levels to play. Players can race one another in any level or climb leaderboards to earn score. Multiplayer is enabled on all levels, granting a full blown MMO experience.

Features

  • Over 65 Fun and Unique Items
    With over 65 items that interact with each other in different ways, you will never run out of ideas when it comes to level creation. An example is the iconic frisbee that can be thrown by the player to be jumped on or used to trigger switches that can turn off conveyors and electric barriers.
  • Fully-Integrated Level Editor
    Transform your idea into a playable level from a level editor packed with content creation tools. The editor can be used to create your own levels or open up any other level.
  • Race mode
    Compete in real-time with your friends to see who can finish the level first! You can invite your friends to race against you.
  • Mobs and the Mob Maker
    With the mob maker, you can make any living creature you'd like. Place the body parts and the mob will come to life. Give it wings and it will fly, give it fins and it will swim!
  • Level browsing & Stars
    Browse the top rated levels easily from anywhere in the game! The best levels naturally come up to the top as players leave stars to the levels they like. The game distinguishes between finished and unfinished creations, preventing the annoyance of playing an unfinished level without knowing it!
  • Controller Support
    All controls are bindable, including ones for the editor!
  • Leaderboards & Stats
    You can bind score by getting top positions on leaderboards of popular levels!
  • Player Customization
    Give a unique look to your character to create your own in-game identity!
  • Replays
    Watch the world record replays to see how they finish so fast! Any replays can be watched from the leaderboard and you can even export them to export videos if you want to show off your skills!

History

Started in late 2016, MakerKing started under the name SocialPlatformer, then changed to Jumpaï and finally settled on MakerKing. I was greatly inspired by Mario Maker at the time but wanted to see how it would play like in a MMO type setting where you see other players around. At first it was only a hobby project I was working on while in college but it slowly grew into what it is today.
