Support us

MakerKing is a two man indie project driven by passion. The game is free to play and contains no ads. If you like MakerKing and want to encourage its development, feel free to support us by purchasing frisbee coins!

Frisbee coins let you buy cosmetics in the shop to decorate your player. This gives no competitive advantage but grants you a lot of style! You can buy them either through the Steam version of the game using your Steam wallet or with Paypal on the itch.io version of the game.

To buy coins, login into the game, click on the account menu (your head in the top right), open the shop and click on the Buy coins button on the bottom left. Cheers!
